Rabbit

Happy rabbits practice a cute behavior known as a binky: they jump up in the air and twist and spin around! A baby rabbit is called a kit, a female is called a doe, and a male is a buck. A group of rabbits is called a herd. Rabbits are herbivores, eating a diet entirely of grasses and other plants

  • Scientific Name--Oryctolagus cuniculus
  • Average length-- 20 — 50 cm
  • Average lifespan-- 3 years
  • Habitat-- meadows, woods, forests, grasslands, deserts and wetlands.

Rabbits are small, furry, mammals with long ears, short fluffy tails, and strong, large hind legs. They have 2 pairs of sharp incisors (front teeth), one pair on top and one pair on the bottom. They also have 2 peg teeth behind the top incisors.
